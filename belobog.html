Belobog

Nearly a millennium ago, the blistering Everwinter buried the world under snow with the arrival of the Eternal Freeze. Belobog survived the disaster, thanks to the blessings from the Preservation. Despite having to weather the harsh climate, humanity persevered and lived to see the sunrise again.

Belobog is a city located in Jarilo-VI. It is divided into two regions: The Overworld and The Underworld.

Overworld

Administrative District

Located at the heart of Belobog, the Administrative District projects its political and cultural influence to the rest of the city. The commands of the Guardian are cascaded down from here to every corner of the city of winter.

Outlying Snow Plains

The Outlying Snow Plains in Honkai: Star Rail are a harsh and unforgiving stretch of land situated on the outskirts of Belobog. This icy region is marked by rugged, snow-covered terrain, steep cliffs, and the constant threat of blizzards, making it a place where survival is a constant struggle.

backwater Pass

Backwater Pass is a desolate and foreboding area within the Underworld of Belobog, a city plagued by internal strife and external threats. This rugged, snow-covered passageway is marked by dilapidated infrastructure, where the harsh realities of the lower class are on full display. The region's bleak environment reflects the ongoing conflict between the city's factions, particularly the struggle between Destruction and Preservation, as well as the encroaching threat of the Antimatter Legion.

Silvermane Guard Restricted Zone

The Silvermane Guard Restricted Zone is an area that is heavily guarded and off-limits to most civilians. It serves as a high-security location within Belobog, likely housing important military facilities, research labs, or areas crucial to the city’s defense efforts. As such, it is a place where many secrets of Belobog are hidden, including sensitive information about its military strategies, the true nature of the Antimatter Legion threat, and perhaps even hidden technologies or artifacts from the past.

Corridor of Fading Echoes

The Corridor of Fading Echoes is a mysterious and emotionally charged location found within Belobog, a city perpetually locked in a struggle for survival against external threats, particularly the Antimatter Legion. The area is symbolic of Belobog's fractured past and present, steeped in forgotten memories, lost stories, and unresolved conflicts. As the name suggests, it is a place where echoes of the past still linger — a corridor that seems to hold the remnants of those who once lived in the city, their lives and struggles fading into obscurity.

Everwinter Hill

Everwinter Hill is a high-altitude, snow-covered location in Belobog, an area dominated by its freezing climate, perpetual winter, and a backdrop of towering mountain ranges. This hill lies on the edge of the Underworld, near the borders of Belobog's harsh and unforgiving terrain, where most of the city's poorest citizens live. It serves as a symbol of both isolation and resilience in Belobog’s fight for survival, with an oppressive atmosphere that reflects the harshness of the environment.

Pillars of Creation

The Pillars of Creation in Belobog are towering, monumental structures located in a key area of the city. These colossal formations stand as both a physical and symbolic reminder of the ancient past and the strength of Belobog’s creation mythos. The pillars are often associated with themes of rebirth, eternity, and divine intervention—ideas that are central to understanding the foundation of Belobog and its fight for survival.

Underworld

Boulder Town

Boulder Town is a tough, industrial area in Belobog, known for its large rock formations and harsh, cold environment. The town is filled with sturdy buildings designed to withstand the freezing temperatures. People here mostly work in mining and industry, extracting resources needed for the city's survival and progress. Despite the harsh conditions, the people of Boulder Town are strong and determined, making it an important part of Belobog's story in Honkai: Star Rail.

Great Mine

The Great Mine is a large, resource-rich area located near Boulder Town in Belobog. It's a key part of the city’s economy, as it supplies vital materials needed for energy and technology. The mine is vast and deep, with workers digging into the earth to extract valuable resources, often under difficult and dangerous conditions. The environment is harsh, with freezing temperatures and constant risks, but the people working there are tough and determined, driven by the need to support the city’s survival. The Great Mine plays a central role in the industrial heart of Belobog, making it a critical location in Honkai: Star Rail.

Rivet Town

Rivet Town is a district in Belobog, known for its industrial atmosphere and mechanical ingenuity. Located near the city's heart, it serves as a hub for manufacturing, repair, and innovation, with factories and workshops filling the area. The town’s name comes from the countless rivets and bolts used in the construction of the massive machines and structures that power Belobog. Despite its gritty, mechanical nature, Rivet Town is crucial to the city’s infrastructure, providing essential services and resources. Its workers, skilled in machinery and craftsmanship, are vital to keeping the city running amidst the harsh cold and constant challenges of survival.

Robot Settlement

the Robot Settlement District in Belobog is a specialized area where advanced robotics play a central role in maintaining the city. This district is filled with high-tech machines and automated systems, ensuring the smooth operation of the settlement amidst the harsh environment. The area is heavily industrial, with large factories and robotic structures that help sustain Belobog's infrastructure. The district's design reflects a blend of technology and necessity, serving as both a hub for mechanical innovation and a critical component for the survival of the city's population in the freezing conditions.

Old Weapon Testing Ground

The Old Weapon Testing Ground in Honkai Star Rail is a desolate and abandoned area within Belobog, where dangerous and experimental weapons were once tested. This area holds a dark history, as it was once used for testing destructive technologies, which are now forgotten and left to rust. Over time, the testing ground has become a neglected and hazardous environment, filled with remnants of old weapons and dormant machinery. Despite its eerie and dangerous nature, the site remains a key location for exploration, often holding secrets and powerful relics that can aid the player’s journey.
